Required Documents

  • Copy of Aadhaar Card - Front and back copies
  • Mobile Number linked with Aadhaar Card - Must be active and accessible
  • Copy of PAN Card - Clear scanned copy
  • Rent Agreement along with NOC - Notarized rental agreement with No Objection Certificate from owner
  • Copy of Aadhaar card of owner - Owner's Aadhaar card for rented property
  • Building receipt of rented property - Latest property tax receipt or ownership proof
  • Utility Bill - Electricity Bill or Gas Bill (not older than 2 months)
  • Passport size photo - Recent color photograph with white background
  • Location details of property - Longitude and latitude of business premises (can be shared via WhatsApp)
  • Product description - Detailed list of goods/services with HSN/SAC codes

Important Notes:

  • All documents must be clear, legible scans (PDF, JPG, or PNG)
  • File size should not exceed 2MB per document
  • Ensure name on all documents matches PAN card
  • Digital signature required for companies and LLPs
